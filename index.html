How it works

VibeCoding layers musical elements that fade in and out to create ever-evolving ambient soundscapes. Toggle stems on/off to control which elements can appear, or adjust their volumes for the perfect mix.Currently in proof-of-concept mode - more keys and probability-based generation coming soon.

Everything stays in sync - no more timing issues when bringing elements in and out.
